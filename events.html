Techcraft Events

Digitrix

Engage in a digital logic and communication adventure within the electronics lab. Solve MOSFET and IC-related tasks in an enjoyable and interactive way.

Winners will be given titles like MOSFET Mastermind and IC Intellect.

Career Simulation (Mock Placement)

Step into the shoes of an electronics core engineer, project manager, or other core roles. Participate in a mock job interview, gaining insights into real-world job scenarios.

ElectroHunt

Embark on a quest to locate electronic equipment based on clues provided. Use your analytical skills to uncover hidden treasures.

Example Clue: "I'm a tiny device with legs of three, In circuits, I'm where electrons want to be. Controlling voltage is my claim to fame, Find me in the lab where circuits play their game. Answer: MOSFET"

EduComix

Educate and entertain as you take on the challenge of teaching a technical topic with a humorous and engaging twist, using memes and creativity to make learning fun.

Shutter UP

Showcase your photography and videography skills in a thrilling competition that combines the art of capturing stills and motion.

Code Crusaders (Bug Bounty)

Dive into coding languages like C, C++, Python, and Java to hunt for and fix bugs. Be rewarded for your coding prowess.

BotExpo

Witness innovation in action as multiple BE projects and robotics creations take center stage in an impressive robot exhibition.

If possible, set up a VR station where participants can experience immersive virtual reality environments or create their own VR content.

Tech Charades

Challenge your technical intuition in a game of charades where you act out complex tech terms and concepts without speaking.

TechQuest

Engage in spirited discussions and debates on technical topics, putting your knowledge and persuasion skills to the test.

In the last round, collaborate with peers to dissect and solve real-world electronic industry challenges in a competitive case study competition in collaboration with a Start-Up.

PCB Wars

Showcase your PCB designing skills in a fierce competition, where precision and creativity reign supreme.

Tech Startup Pitch

Students with entrepreneurial aspirations pitch their tech startup ideas to a panel of judges, similar to the TV show "Shark Tank." The most innovative and feasible ideas win.

Tech Talk Show

A live talk show featuring a guest speaker from the tech industry, discussing the latest trends, innovations, and career opportunities in tech.
