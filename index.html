About TESA

The Electronics and Telecommunication Students' Association (TESA) at PVG's College of Engineering and Technology is dedicated to fostering innovation, learning, and community engagement within the field of Electronics and Telecommunication.

Previously by TESA

  1. Traditional Day and Farewell Party (May 10, 2022)

    TESA organized a memorable Traditional Day and Farewell Party for the BE E & TC students, creating lasting memories and celebrating their journey.

  2. Technovation 2K22 (May 6-7, 2022)

    TESA hosted a National-level Circuit Design and Simulation Competition and Business Ideation Competition, welcoming students from SE, TE, and BE E&T C programs. Distinguished judges, Mr. Bhavin Shah and Mr. Akhilesh Deo, evaluated the projects.

  3. Versatalia - IEEE Project Competition (April 30, 2022)

    In collaboration with the Electrical and IT Department students' association, TESA organized the National-Level IEEE Project Competition, Versatalia, showcasing innovative projects and ideas.

  4. Faculty Badminton Competition (October 11, 2021)

    TESA organized a friendly badminton competition for the faculty members of the E & TC department, promoting sportsmanship and camaraderie.

  5. Group Discussion Competition (August 28, 2021)

    TESA hosted a thought-provoking Group Discussion competition for students, fostering an environment of meaningful dialogue and intellectual exchange.

  6. Blood Donation Camp (August 14, 2021)

    In collaboration with the NSS unit, TESA organized a Blood Donation Camp, contributing to a noble cause and promoting community welfare.
